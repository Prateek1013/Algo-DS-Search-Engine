
CSES - Christmas Party


Time limit: 1.00 s
Memory limit: 512 MB



There are nn children at a Christmas party, and each of them has brought a gift. The idea is that everybody will get a gift brought by someone else.

In how many ways can the gifts be distributed?

Input

The only input line has an integer nn: the number of children.

Output

Print the number of ways modulo 109+7109+7.

Constraints

1≤n≤1061≤n≤106

Example

Input:
4

Output:
9
