
CSES - Advertisement


Time limit: 1.00 s
Memory limit: 512 MB



A fence consists of nn vertical boards. The width of each board is 1 and their heights may vary.

You want to attach a rectangular advertisement to the fence. What is the maximum area of such an advertisement?

Input

The first input line contains an integer nn: the width of the fence.

After this, there are nn integers k1,k2,…,knk1,k2,…,kn: the height of each board.

Output

Print one integer: the maximum area of an advertisement.

Constraints

1≤n≤2⋅1051≤n≤2⋅105
1≤ki≤1091≤ki≤109

Example

Input:
8
4 1 5 3 3 2 4 1

Output:
10
